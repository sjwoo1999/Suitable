# Image Generation Prompts for Suitable

These prompts are designed to generate high-quality, photorealistic images that align with the "Suitable" brand identity (Premium, Navy/Mint, Professional).

## 1. Couple Image (Individual & Friends)
**Concept:** A stylish young couple (or two friends) looking confident and happy in their rented suits.
**Prompt:**
> A stylish young Korean couple in their 20s, wearing matching premium navy tailored suits. They are walking confidently side-by-side on a modern, clean city street during the day. Soft, natural sunlight. They are smiling and looking at each other or ahead. The vibe is sophisticated, professional, yet energetic. High fashion photography style, cinematic depth of field, 8k resolution. Subtle mint color accents in accessories (tie or scarf).

## 2. Group Image (Group Packages)
**Concept:** A group of students or young professionals showing team spirit and the scale of the service.
**Prompt:**
> A diverse group of 5-6 young Korean university students or fresh graduates, all wearing sharp, well-fitted navy and charcoal grey suits. They are standing together outdoors on a university campus or modern business district, laughing and talking naturally. Dynamic composition, not a stiff pose. Bright, energetic atmosphere. The image conveys "teamwork" and "success". Photorealistic, high detail, 8k.

## 3. VIP Card Image (VIP Campus Pass)
**Concept:** A premium, exclusive membership card that feels tangible and valuable.
**Prompt:**
> A close-up, macro shot of a premium membership card resting on a dark, textured surface (like slate or leather). The card is matte black with deep navy undertones. It features gold foil embossed text saying "VIP" and the "Suitable" logo. Elegant, dramatic lighting with gold reflections. Shallow depth of field to focus on the card texture. Luxurious, exclusive, high-end product photography style. 8k.

## 4. Delivery/Pickup Station at Korea Univ
**Concept:** A pop-up pickup station on campus where students are queuing to receive their premium suit boxes from a manager.
**Prompt:**
> A pop-up pickup station set up outdoors on the Korea University campus, with the iconic gothic stone buildings in the background. A professional staff member wearing a navy apron is handing a premium navy suit box to a student. A line of 3-4 stylish students is waiting in an orderly queue to pick up their rentals. There is a small, elegant sign that says "Suitable Pickup". The atmosphere is organized, premium, and convenient. Soft afternoon sunlight, photorealistic, 8k.
